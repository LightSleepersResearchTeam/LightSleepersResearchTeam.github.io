プラハイウェイNO.1



Provider: LS_Vio

The most basic set in the entire series. Only 2 complete layouts could be assembled, leaving the slope rails unused for open-ended layouts. Unfortunately it has no ちょくせんレール, so it limits the possibilities of assembling layouts. Therefore, this results in ちょくせんレール being rarer than the さかレール. Rail guards and grey streetlamps make their appearance in this set. The included car can be a Corona 1600S Hardtop (Matte Red / Metallic Red), or a Bluebird 510 (Metallic Red), but the Bluebird 510 is an ultra phantom class rarity as at least 3 of the variants only appeared back in 2016. Someday a Metallic Red Bluebird will need to be collected for research purposes. This set comes in 4 versions: Blue interior tray, green interior tray, blue box lid side, and green box lid side. This product I have has a factory print error, as the yellow out-of-bounds are showing and the box illustration print is a little misplaced. One of two types of きょくせんレール could be found in this set: Oval outer holes, or circular outer holes. 100km/h speed limit signs are included in this generation of Pla Highway but not in the newer set item releases, this actually correctly coincides with the fact that the cars manufactured in this generation (double AA batteries) runs faster than the newer generation of Pla Highway cars (single AA battery).

This review was written by LS_Vio.





プラハイウェイNO.2



Provider: LS_Vio

Finally a Pla Highway set with ちょくせんレール and ポイント部品, so it feels more complete with more layout possibilities than プラハイウェイNO.1. It definitely feels like a more luxurious version of プラハイウェイNO.1. However it is lacking はしげた・つなぎ部品, which I feel is a real shame since はしげた・つなぎ部品 can only be obtained as a single item. There are just enough rails and piers to assemble your dream layout. Take note of the one and only included double overhead signboard, as this is a masterpiece of a design in my opinion. ポイントレール and the overhead signboards pave the initial road for the Double Highway product line. Just like プラハイウェイNO.1, there are 4 types of product variants: Blue interior tray, green interior tray, blue box lid sides, and green box lid sides. Also, 3 possible types of cars: Corona 1600S Hardtop (Matte Red / Metallic Red), and Bluebird 510 (Mettalic Red). This set contains the metallic red version of the Corona 1600S. You might have noticed that metallic red cars are the first generation cars in the series, and they have a different dolly than their matte red counterparts.

This complete review was written by LS_Vio.



Provider: LS_なみだ9

おじいちゃんが住んでいた町ではこのセットがとても人気があったそうです。おじいちゃんから譲り受けたセットにはマット赤色のコロナ1600Sがあるので1969年の第2ロットで製造されたものです。1968年製造の初回ロットはメタリック赤色のコロナ1600Sが付属しています。2種類の車の台車も微妙に違う。この時代に製造された車は1970年代に製造された車に比べはるかにスピードが速い。きょくせんレールの内側穴が楕円形になっている別種類のセットもありますが、ここでは丸い穴になっています。ポイントレールのレバーが着脱式なのが良いです。公式レイアウトはかなり広いんです。看板や街灯などはシールの関係で少し剥がしにくいのが気になります。経年劣化を防ぐために全てのシールにラミネート加工が施されていればと思いました。要約すると、プラハイウェイNO.1を大きくしたものです。
